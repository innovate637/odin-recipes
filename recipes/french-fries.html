French fries

Description

Side dish or snack typically made from deep-fried potatoes that

have been cut into various shapes, especially thin strips.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Cut the Potatoes

    Use a sharp knife to cut each peeled potato into slices,

    then sticks.

  2. Soak and Drain the Potatoes

    Soak the potato sticks in a bowl of cold water

    for about 5 minutes. Drain, then pour boiling water over

    the potatoes. Let sit for about 10 minutes. Drain and dry the potatoes.

  3. Season the French Fries

  4. Cook In Air Fryer
